WHAT IS NaCl

NaCl is the chemical formula for common table salt, also known as sodium chloride. It's composed of sodium (Na) and chlorine (Cl) ions, and it's a crucial compound for various biological processes in the human body. Sodium chloride is widely used in cooking to enhance flavor, as well as in food preservation and many industrial processes
